This prototype turns source-backed MLIR research into an article-first reading surface with a concept rail, normalized data contract, and visualization lenses for structure, flow, symbols, and pass scope.

Input Official MLIR docs

Process Chain

  1. Collect authoritative MLIR sources.
  2. Extract structural facts and terminology.
  3. Normalize entities, edges, and snapshots.
  4. Project the same model into multiple views.

Entry

Why This Reading Surface Exists

MLIR cannot be represented faithfully by a single dashboard or a single graph. The official documentation defines a recursively nested IR with operations, regions, blocks, values, symbol tables, and operation-scoped pass pipelines. This page treats the article as the product and keeps the concept layer visible as a secondary apparatus instead of hiding it behind separate glossary pages.

The result is a concrete end-to-end review surface: the raw source material is visible, the knowledge cut is explicit, and the visualization layer is derived from a normalized contract instead of being drawn freehand.
